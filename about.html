about our team

They say that one man's junk is another man's treasure. This is the exact principle that we apply when we collect any junk from you. We work with a handful of charities that make use of the unwanted items that we collect from our clients. In this way, we do all that we can to uplift and enrich the community in which we work. If you would like more information about the charities with whom we work, feel free to get in touch with us using the number at the top of this page. If you feel that your unwanted items are not in the condition to be repurposed, do not fret! We still ensure that the items are put to good use. Whatever items we cannot give to charity, we recycle. This means that when you work with LA Junk & Removal, you make an ethical choice!

Our team has been in this industry for decades and loves helping our customers declutter, destress, and make their homes healthier, happier places. This is something we love doing. We know that many people can become buried under mountains of old, useless items which they want to get rid of, but do not have a clue where to start. We assist in this regard not only with our junk removal services but also with our cleaning services, so that we can help you sort through the mess - no matter how big or small!
